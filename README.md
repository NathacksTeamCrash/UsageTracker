## A wellness app that helps you save money and the planet üåç

EcoLog an app designed to help reduce electricity and water consumption every month with personalized tracking, custom goals and a point system with "Eco Points". 
By answering a short questionnaire of their home size, historic usage and sustainability goals, users can create easily tracked and actionable goals like shortening their shower length to earn Eco Points, motivating behaviour changes for a more environmentally sutainable lifestyle.

## Features:
- Leaderboard: Encourages lower usage in communities
- Progress Tracking: Visualizes monthly usage
- Eco Points: Rewards users for completing tasks and reducing usage
- Goal Setting: Customizable goals

## Motivation:
Overconsumption has become a prevalent issue, resulting in significant environmental impacts and bills constantly getting higher. By tracking usage, EcoLog motivates users to "beat" previous months by making sustainable living seem more in reach and achieveable.

## Usage:
- Sign up and fill out the short questionnaire
- Set your goals for reducing consumption
- Complete tasks daily or weekly like keeping showers under 15 minutes
- Earn eco points by completing those tasks
- Adjust goals by your performance
